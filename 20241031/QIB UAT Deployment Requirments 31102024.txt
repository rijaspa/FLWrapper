The error "Object already exists" could occur if the RSA key is corrupted. To fix this, try deleting and recreating the key.


If you find a conflicting key, delete it using:
aspnet_regiis -pz "NetFrameworkConfigurationKey"

Recreate the key:
aspnet_regiis -pc "NetFrameworkConfigurationKey" -exp
